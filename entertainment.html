Entertainment News

_____________________________________________________________________

Entertainment in the Philippines is vibrant and diverse, featuring a mix of music, film, TV dramas, pageants, and digital content. It blends local culture with global trends, with celebrities, love teams, and talent shows playing key roles in shaping pop culture.





Jessica Sanchez returns to 'American's Got Talent' stage after 20 years

Filipino-Mexican singer Jessica Sanchez is set to return to "America's Got Talent" (AGT) in its upcoming season, nearly 20 years after first joining the show at age 10. In a trailer, she appears before judges Simon Cowell, Howie Mandel, Sofia Vergara, and Mel B, sharing how AGT had a major impact on her early career. Jessica, who reached the AGT semifinals in 2006 and later became the runner-up on "American Idol" Season 11, expressed her excitement on Instagram about chasing her dreams again. She released her debut album Me, You and the Music in 2013, featuring a duet with Ne-Yo.


BINI Gwen and BINI Jhoana graduate from senior high.

BINI members Jhoanna Robles and Gwen Apuli recently graduated from senior high school at the Japanese-Philippines Institute of Technologies, Training, and Services Inc. They also received Youth Leadership Excellence awards. Both expressed pride in their accomplishments despite the challenges of balancing school with their music careers. Jhoanna will pursue Mass Communications in college, while Gwen plans to study Interior Design. They emphasized the importance of having a Plan B and fulfilling their parents' dream for them to graduate.


Cinema One to screen Nora Aunor, Mother's Day films this May

In May, Cinema One will honor the late Nora Aunor, a legendary actress and National Artist, with a special film festival. The channel will feature three of her iconic movies during its Restored Cinema segment: Minsa'y Isang Gamu-gamo (May 11), Bulaklak sa City Jail (May 18), and Himala (May 25), all airing at 10 p.m. Additionally, the channel will showcase Mother's Day tributes at 3 p.m., including Madrasta, One More Try, and Anak. On Mondays, Cinema One will also air Judy Ann Santos movies such as Kahit Isang Saglit and Sabel.











Heart Evangelista's advice to fellow 'artistas' afraid to come out with their 'retoke'

Heart Evangelista recently spoke openly about cosmetic procedures during an Ultherapy event, encouraging fellow celebrities to be honest about their aesthetic journeys. She admitted that while rumors about her getting procedures like eye or nose surgeries have circulated, she prefers transparency to avoid "fake news." At 40, Heart emphasized the importance of being open about cosmetic enhancements, especially in the age of social media.


Maki share why UP is very close to his heart, shows excitement for upcoming album

Saan hitmaker Maki shared his deep connection to the University of the Philippines (UP), describing it as a place where he seeks solace during both happy and down moments. In an interview before his performance at the “UP Fair: REV Music Festival,” he revealed that UP holds a special place in his heart. When asked about his career and love life, Maki described his career as "dilaw" (yellow), symbolizing brightness and growth, especially after overcoming periods of sadness. He also humorously shared that he is currently focused on self-love and personal growth, not having a romantic relationship. Maki expressed his excitement for the release of his full-length album KOLORCOASTER, promising surprises for his fans with songs that are truly meaningful to him.
